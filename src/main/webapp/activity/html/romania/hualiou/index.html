About us
Wenzhou Hualiou Shoes Co., Ltd. was established in 2011, and its predecessor Wenzhou Lucheng Leather Shoes Factory was established in 2007. Hualiou Shoes is a modern shoe-making enterprise integrating R&D, production and sales. With three advanced shoe-making lines, the annual production of 1.8 million pairs of women's leather shoes, the production and sales of "Hualiou" women's shoes, favored by the majority of customers. Since its inception, the company has always adhered to the business philosophy of "people-oriented, steady development, excellence, scientific innovation". Based on instinct, opening up the market, and facing the opportunities and challenges of economic globalization, the company actively responded to the country's "going out" strategy. And achieved brilliant results.
