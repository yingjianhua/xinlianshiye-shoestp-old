About us
　　Disheng Shoes Industry Co., Ltd. was founded in 1996 (formerly known as Aoerkang Shoes Industry Factory). In the early stage of the development from 1996 to 2000, the company served many well-known domestic shoe brands, such as Septwolves in Fujian. In 2001, the company valued the product development of customers in overseas markets and sought the transformation of the company. Up to now, the company has carried out many years of friendly cooperation with many well-known foreign brands. In 2008, the company moved to Panqiao Street in Ouhai District and officially changed its name to Disheng Shoes Industry Co., Ltd. The factory building area is 8856 square meters where one factory building and one staff quarter were built. The company has advanced production lines, years of production experience and strong technical capabilities. In the 20 years of development, the company adheres to the principle of closely cooperating with customers, helping to solve technical problems, and strictly controlling the quality of products to serve customers. Received high praise from customers in Europe, America, Japan, and Russia. From the small workshop operation to the construction of the company system, Disheng Footwear Co., Ltd emphasizes the four standards of management execution which include product creativity, employee cooperation and production standardization.
　　Disheng Shoes Industry Co., Ltd. adheres to the principles of tolerance, fairness, and justice, persisting in encouraging its workers to be creative and hardworking.
