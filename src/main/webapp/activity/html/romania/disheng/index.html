About us
Disheng Shoes was established in 1996. From 1996 to 2000, it mainly became a domestic women's shoe. In 2001, it began to import and export women's shoes and children's shoes. The factory is located in Building 5, Hengtang Industrial Zone, Panqiao Town, Ouhai District, Wenzhou City. It covers an area of 6-7 mu. It has a factory building and a dormitory building. The factory has advanced production equipment and strong technical capabilities. It is committed to exquisite design, persistent quality, and conscientiously cooperate with accurate delivery and other good services, resulting in high evaluation and praise from customers. The number of employees in the factory has increased from more than 30 people in the beginning to more than 500 people in the peak period. They have understanding, fairness and justice, adhere to principles, persistent requirements, encourage creation and mutual encouragement!
