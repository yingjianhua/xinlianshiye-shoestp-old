About us
Founded in 2006, Zhejiang Jiebaqiao Shoes Co., Ltd., formerly known as Rui'an Zelin Footwear Co., Ltd., is located in Wenzhou, one of the most economically active cities in China. It is a professional manufacturer of high quality safety shoes, protective shoes and work shoes. A series of modern integrated enterprises integrating development, production and sales. Zhejiang Jieba Bacho Shoes Co., Ltd. adheres to the production philosophy of “advocating science and technology, people-oriented, pursuing excellence” and the management tenet of “quality first, customer first”.
